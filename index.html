Problem Statement

Many students face a tough battle with mental health, and it's time we understand their challenges. Lack of
awareness and stigma surrounding mental health is real and often force students into silence, making it hard to
seek professional help. Access to mental health professionals is limited, and when available, the quality of care is
often expensive and may not meet the diverse needs of students.

We are on a mission to change the narrative around students’ mental health, address their struggles through
awareness, education, provide resources and empower them to thrive in the face of challenges. We envision
creating a community where every student can flourish emotionally, academically, and socially.
